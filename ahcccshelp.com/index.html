Good to know

Questions we hear a lot

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both work. Book online in under a minute for a guaranteed time, or simply walk in — we welcome walk-ins at both locations and offer same-day appointments whenever possible.

What insurance do you accept?

Most major insurance plans — plus AHCCCS, Medicare, WIC and workers' comp. Don't see your plan? Call (623) 846-7597 — chances are we accept it.

Can I get a same-day ultrasound?

Often, yes — same-day and walk-in ultrasounds are available subject to availability. Booking online or calling ahead is the fastest way to be seen the same day.

Is my visit confidential?

Completely. Every visit is 100% private and judgment-free — including care for teens.
